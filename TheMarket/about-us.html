About Us

Our Mission

A marketplace to access radical buying power from capitalizing on deep employee discounts and friends/family discounts

About

Utilizing the decentralized nature of blockchain technology, DEEP facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers via secure smart contracts. Buyers will have access to deep discounts and sellers will be incentivized to offer there discounts.

Imagine a gateway that grants direct access to thousands of insider discounts.

Features

Ethereum Blockchain

Utilize the Ethereum blockchain to facilitate payments and leverage smart contracts.

Ethereum Smart Contracts

Smart Contracts ensures that parties have to deliver their end of the deal or else the transaction will not go through, protecting both parties.

Double Deposit Escrow

Double Escrow requires both parties to set aside another pool of funds to ensure the deal go through and is refunded after the transaction is complete. This ANOTHER layer of security to protect users from fraudulent deals.

Confido

Confido provides secure shipment tracking to help retail associates to check if their item has been delivered to the buyer. Buyers and sellers can also freeze the payments if there is a conflict in the delivered item and come to a settlement.

Whisper - Anonymous Chat

Whisper facilitates anonymous chat. Users will be no more than numbers to other users.
